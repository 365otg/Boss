A Program Unlike Any Other

B.O.S.S. (Business of Sports Systems) is a one-of-a-kind program created to help student-athletes transform their passion for sports into meaningful careers beyond high school, extending far beyond just being a player.

Through workshops, discussions, and visits to local and regional sites, students explore a variety of career paths within the sports industry, gaining firsthand exposure to high-level roles and professional opportunities.
